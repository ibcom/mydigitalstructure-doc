We have created a mydigitalstructure.com special offer for Windows 8 camp developers, this is similar to the existing BizSpark offer.


If you're a developer..

We have removed the data record limit on the developer version.

You can use mydigitalstructure.com to play with or organise your own business forever, for free*.

Standard pricing is $40/user/month,
so over 1 year and for 1 user the value is $480.

The more users and the longer you use it of course the greater the value!

There's no limit on users that are within your development organisation. 

Build as many apps as you like within your space.  A great way to develop and demonstrate your apps.

mydigitalstucture.com is a hosted organisational REST API, with over 500 methods for managing:

  • Websites
  • Contacts
  • Actions/Activity (including time billing)
  • Messaging (conversations, IMAP integration)
  • Projects
  • Documents
  • Products, Orders & Stock
  • Full Accounting with tax reporting.
    including full BAS and Payroll in Australia.

With full OAuth and OAuth2.0 support you can also extend the model into facebook, twitter, linkedin and other webapi's.

If you're familiar with the model-view-controller (MVC) framework you'll love mydigitalstructure.com and if not, it's a great way to get started.
