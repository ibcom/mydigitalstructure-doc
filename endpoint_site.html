LINKING A DOMAIN TO A SITE
  The CNAME for your site to be handled by the mydigitalstructure platform is:

site.mydigitalstructure.com
 

To associate the domain with a site use:

SETUP_SITE_URL_MANAGE
SETUP_SITE_URL_SEARCH
