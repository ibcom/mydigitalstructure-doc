A STEP BACK
Traditionally when trying to understand the universe in engineering terms, it has been all about "matter & energy" - now "data" (information) is emerging as a first class citizen when trying to understand the world around us.

More reading on information & energy/matter

Everyday we use information to manage matter and energy - there is a deep inter-relation.
REST
We use information to hold the represent the real world (its state) - the internet uses the commonality of the http protocol to transfer this representational state, using some basic verbs: GET, POST, DELETE to change the state of the representational information (data) - known as "RE.S.T"

With a common set of objects (nouns) i.e. contact, project, issues etc hosted by mydigitalstructure.com, you can represent the state of an organisation - being the combination of people, purpose, process & product.
MAKING IT REAL
Byunderstanding the mydigitalstructure objects and the methods (functions), you can then design and build "information machines/engines" - that can be used to solve real-world human problems - the nature of all technology.
