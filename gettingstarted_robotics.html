GETTING STARTED
ROBOTICS & TELEMETRY

The mydigitalstructure service can be used in 2 ways: 

  • Gathering information from the real world (telemetry) and using that to augment business processes
  • Sending information to control real world devices (robots) bsaed on business information changes.

 

 

TELEMETRY

Using wireless or fixed wire communication any device that is RESTful compliant (ie can send a message over HTTPS) can be mashed into an onDemand based application.

 

 

TELEMETRY

Telemetry is a technology that allows remote measurement and reporting of information. The word is derived from Greek roots tele = remote, and metron = measure.
