mydigitalspace > mydigitalstructure
29 Nov 2011
mark.byers

Hi all,

To better represent and communicate the ibCom service and reflect what has been our role over the last 11 years, we have started the process of transitioning from the name "mydigitalspace" to "mydigitalstructure".

In the sense that ibCom provides the digital structures of which our partners use to create digital organisational information spaces.

In the same way that builders use steel, wood & concrete structures to build human living spaces.

In is only s slight tweak and wont have any really impact on existing services - other than the name and focusing the service around the mydigitalstructure.com domain going forward.   The mydigitalspace domains will all remain active well into the future.
